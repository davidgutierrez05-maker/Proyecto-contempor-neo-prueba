Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

Contemporánica collects information you provide directly to us when you create an account, such as your name, email address, artistic role, and any professional data you choose to share in your profile.

2. Use of Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, facilitate professional networking between composers and performers, and personalize your experience on our platform.

3. Data Sharing

Your profile information is public to other registered users of the platform to facilitate professional discovery. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your information at any time through your profile settings. We retain your data as long as your account is active.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including authentication via Supabase, to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.
